FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MandarinCourse really free?
Yes! HSK 1 is completely free with access to AI tutor, flashcards, writing practice, and all features. Premium access unlocks HSK 2-9 and unlimited AI conversations for $9/month.
Do I need to create an account?
No account needed to start. You can learn HSK 1 immediately. Create a free account to sync your progress across devices and save your study data.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes! MandarinCourse is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Open it in any browser on any device. You can also install it to your home screen for a native app experience.
How does the AI tutor work?
Our AI tutor Li Laoshi listens to your Chinese via microphone or text input. You get real-time corrections, pinyin tips, grammar explanations, and natural conversation practice. The AI adapts to your level.
What HSK levels are covered?
HSK 1 through HSK 9. Each level has vocabulary lists, dialogue lessons, grammar patterns, quizzes, and writing characters. Premium gives you access to all levels.
